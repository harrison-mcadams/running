How to Make Access Durable (Prevent 7-Day Expiry)

Google OAuth tokens in "Testing" mode expire after 7 days. To make your access permanent:

  1. Go to the Google Cloud Console.
  2. Select your project and navigate to APIs & Services > OAuth consent screen.
  3. Under "Publishing status", click the Publish App button and confirm.

This transitions your app to "In Production", which keeps the refresh token active permanently (no verification review is needed for personal apps!).
